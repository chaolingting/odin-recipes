Cheese cake

Ingredients

Crust:

  • Digestive biscuits – 120g
  • Unsalted butter (melted) – 60g

Cheese filling:

  • Cream cheese – 400g
  • Sugar – 100g
  • Eggs – 2
  • Sour cream – 120g
  • Vanilla extract – 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice – 1 tbsp(Optional)

Instructions

  1. Crush biscuits, mix with melted butter, press into a pan, and chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Beat softened cream cheese until smooth, then add sugar and mix well.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. Mix in sour cream, vanilla extract, and lemon juice until combined.
  5. Pour filling onto the crust and smooth the top.
  6. Bake at 160°C (320°F) for 50–60 mins, use a water bath to prevent cracking.
  7. Cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
